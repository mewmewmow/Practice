# Business Model & Financial Projections

## Executive Summary

SmartBook is a B2B SaaS platform targeting small businesses (salons, fitness studios, consultants, trades).

**Market Opportunity:**
- TAM (Total Addressable Market): 28M small businesses globally
- SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market): 5M (in English-speaking markets)
- SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market): Year 1 target = 500 customers

---

## Pricing Strategy

### Three-Tier Model

| Metric | Free | Pro | Enterprise |
|--------|------|-----|------------|
| **Price** | $0 | $29/mo | $99/mo |
| **Bookings/mo** | 10 | 500 | Unlimited |
| **Services** | 1 | 10 | Unlimited |
| **Payment Collection** | Manual | Automated | Custom |
| **Team Members** | 1 | 3 | 10+ |
| **Analytics** | Basic | Advanced | Advanced |
| **Support** | Community | Priority | Dedicated |

### Rationale
- **Free**: Activation funnel, low friction
- **Pro**: Best for SME sweet spot, recurring revenue
- **Enterprise**: Lock-in for established businesses

### Pricing Expansion (Year 2)
- Add-on: SMS Reminders ($5/mo)
- Add-on: Email Marketing ($10/mo)
- Add-on: Custom Domain ($3/mo)

## Unit Economics

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)

**Scenario 1: High touch sales**
- Sales person: $3,000/mo
- Closes: 10 customers/mo
- CAC: $300/customer

**Scenario 2: Content driven**
- Monthly marketing budget: $500
- Conversion rate: 2% of traffic
- Traffic needed: 500 visitors → 10 signups → 2 conversions
- CAC: $250/customer

**Scenario 3: Viral/Referral**
- Viral coefficient: 1.2 (each customer brings 1.2 more)
- Organic CAC: $50/customer (reduced marketing needed)

### Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)

**Pro Customer:**
- Monthly revenue: $29
- Avg. retention: 18 months (3-month implied churn rate 8%)
- LTV: $29 × 18 = $522
- LTV:CAC ratio: $522/$250 = 2.1x ✓

**Enterprise Customer:**
- Monthly revenue: $99
- Avg. retention: 36 months
- LTV: $99 × 36 = $3,564
- LTV:CAC ratio: $3,564/$300 = 11.9x ✓✓

## Growth Strategies (Unit Economics)

### Strategy 1: Bottom-up SEM (Content Marketing)
- Build SEO: "appointment booking for [industry]"
- Timeline: 3-6 months for organic traffic
- Cost: $200/mo content writer
- Expected: 100-200 organic visits/mo → 5% signup = 5-10 signups/mo
- Cost per signup: $40-50

### Strategy 2: Direct Sales
- Hire sales person part-time ($2,000/mo)
- Close rate: 20-30% of meetings
- Meetings booked: 30/mo
- Closings: 6-9/mo
- Cost per customer: $250-330

### Strategy 3: Tech-Based Growth
- Zapier integration (list on marketplace)
- Product Hunt launch
- B2B marketplaces (Capterra, G2)
- No additional cost, organic growth

## Sensitivity Analysis

### What if churn doubles (from 8% to 16%)?
- Year 1 customers: 150 → 120
- Year 1 MRR: $4,870 → $3,750
- Year 1 Profit: $21,180 → $8,940
- **Action:** Focus on retention, reduce paid marketing

### What if we get 2x traffic but 0.5x conversion?
- Same customer acquisition overall
- Higher brand awareness
- Long-term positive impact
- **Action:** Continue content strategy

### What if conversion rate improves 50% (current 2% → 3%)?
- Year 1 customers: 150 → 225
- Year 1 MRR: $4,870 → $7,305
- Year 1 revenue: $58,440 → $87,660
- **Action:** This is the highest-leverage metric to optimize
