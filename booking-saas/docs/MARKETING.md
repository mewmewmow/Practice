# Marketing & Client Acquisition Strategy

## 🎯 Marketing Channels (Priority Order)

### 1. Content Marketing (Highest ROI, Long-term)
Target keywords:
- "Online booking system for [industry]"
- "Appointment scheduling software"
- "Free booking calendar"

**Content Ideas:**
1. "Salon Owners: 5 Reasons for Online Booking" (blog post)
2. "How to Reduce No-Shows by 40%" (guide)
3. "Appointment Scheduling Comparison" (vs Calendly, Acuity)
4. "Getting Started with SmartBook" (video tutorial)

**Timeline:** 3-6 months for SEO results

### 2. Direct Sales (High Conversion Rate)
**Target:** Salon owners, fitness centers, consulting practices

**Outreach Template:**
```
Subject: [Business Name] - Quick idea for more bookings

Hi [Owner Name],

I noticed you offer [service]. Many [business type] are now using 
online booking and seeing a 30-40% increase in appointments.

SmartBook is a simple online booking system - no tech needed.
- Free for first 10 bookings
- Takes 5 minutes to setup
- [Link to demo]

Would you be open to a quick 10-min call to see if it could help?

Best,
[Your Name]
```

### 3. Partnership & Affiliates (Scalable)
**Partners:**
- Web designers
- POS system providers
- Industry consultants
- Web hosting companies

**Commission Structure:** 30% recurring (split 70/30 after month 1)

### 4. Paid Ads (Quick initial growth)
**Platforms:**
- Google Ads (Search) - $2-5 CPC
- Facebook Ads - $1-3 CPC
- LinkedIn - $3-8 CPC

**Recommended Budget:** $500-1000/month initially

**Google Ads Copy Example:**
```
Headline: Free Online Booking for Your Business
Description: No setup fee. No credit card. Start scheduling online in 5 min
Domain: smartbook.com
CTA: Start Free Now
```

### 5. Community & Networking
- Reddit communities: r/smallbusiness, r/entrepreneur
- LinkedIn groups
- Facebook groups (industry-specific)
- Local chamber of commerce

## 🎬 Sales Funnel Optimization

### Customer Journey Map

```
Awareness (Discovery)
    ↓
    ├─ Organic Search (30% of traffic)
    ├─ Content Recommendations (25%)
    ├─ Direct (20%)
    └─ Paid Ads (25%)
    ↓
Interest (Landing Page)
    ↓
    ├─ Free Trial Signup: 8-12% conversion
    ├─ Demo Request: 2-3% conversion
    └─ Bounce: 85%
    ↓
Consideration (During Free Trial)
    ↓
    ├─ Create 1st booking: 40% of signups
    ├─ Invite team members: 20%
    ├─ Never logged in: 40%
    ↓
Decision (Upgrade)
    ↓
    ├─ Convert to Pro: 15-25% of active trials
    └─ Remain Free: 75-85%
    ↓
Retention
    ├─ Pro retention after 1 mo: 85%
    ├─ Pro retention after 3 mo: 70%
    └─ Pro churn after 6 mo: 15-20%
```

### Optimization Targets
- Increase landing page conversion: 10% → 15%
- Reduce free-to-paid: 25% → 40%
- Reduce 3-month churn: 20% → 10%
